Our Story

Why we built this

Service providers were spending too much time on recruiting and too little on growth. The driver qualification process is detailed, time-sensitive, and compliance-critical — and most operators don't have an HR team built for it.

Mountain Group was founded to be that team. We know the compliance requirements, the platform workflows, and the pressure of keeping routes staffed. We handle the pipeline so you can handle the business.

Today we serve ISPs across the country, with US leadership and a dedicated team that moves as fast as your routes demand.

Why did Mountain Group build a recruiting firm specifically for logistics service providers?
+

Most staffing agencies are generalist. Carriers and contractors needed a recruiting partner who understood their specific compliance requirements, timelines, and the operational pressure of keeping routes staffed. Mountain Group was built exclusively for that purpose — to be the recruiting infrastructure that logistics providers don't have in-house.

Are you affiliated with FedEx or Amazon?
+

No. Mountain Group LLC is an independent recruiting services company. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, approved by, or sponsored by Federal Express Corporation, FedEx Ground, or Amazon. These names are referenced solely to describe the contractors and carriers we serve.

Where is the Mountain Group team based?
+

Mountain Group is headquartered in the United States. Our leadership and core team are based here and handle strategy, compliance, and all client relationships. We also operate a back-office support team in India that keeps the recruiting pipeline running around the clock — so your candidates are being worked 24 hours a day, not just during US business hours. All personnel use named accounts with enforced MFA on company-controlled encrypted devices.

How long has Mountain Group been operating?
+

We have been serving carriers and contractors since our founding, helping hundreds of operators qualify drivers and fill routes across the country. Our team has screened thousands of candidates across multiple states.

Do you work with Amazon DSPs as well as FedEx contractors?
+

Our primary focus and deepest expertise is in FedEx contractor driver qualification. Contact us to discuss your specific operation — we evaluate fit on a case-by-case basis.

How do you handle sensitive candidate data?
+

Candidate profiles are initiated with only name and contact information. Every candidate enters their own sensitive identifiers — SSN, date of birth — directly into the platform's secure, encrypted portal. Mountain Group personnel never handle, store, or transmit this information. All company devices are fully encrypted and protected with enforced multi-factor authentication.

What states do you recruit in?
+

We recruit nationally. We've qualified drivers for service providers across dozens of states. Our sourcing is not geography-limited — we find candidates wherever your routes are.

Can I see candidate status without asking your team?
+

Yes. We provide real-time pipeline visibility and monthly KPI reporting as part of every engagement. You can see the status of background checks, drug tests, and physical exams without making a single call — and we proactively flag any issues before you need to ask.

What happens if a candidate fails their background check or drug test?
+

We immediately re-enter the sourcing and screening pipeline to replace them — that's included in your engagement. Adverse action procedures are handled through the platform's built-in compliant process. You don't have to manage any part of that.

Can I pause and restart the service anytime?
+

Yes. Month-to-month, no long-term commitment, pause anytime. When you restart, budget about 12 days to rebuild an active pipeline. Our most effective clients run the service continuously to keep a bench of candidates ready — so a driver quitting doesn't mean scrambling from zero.
