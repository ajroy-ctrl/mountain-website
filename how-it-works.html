Who We Screen In — and Who We Filter Out

We're looking for people who already know how to work hard

FedEx P&D delivery is physically demanding. During every phone screen we're explicit with candidates: this job means bending and lifting hundreds of times a day, driving a large vehicle in all weather, and staying on your feet through a full shift. That honesty is what keeps the pipeline clean.

✓
We actively recruit
  • ✓ Warehouse workers & laborers
  • ✓ Roofers, plumbers, HVAC technicians
  • ✓ Movers & construction workers
  • ✓ Firefighters & first responders
  • ✓ Anyone with a history of hard physical work
✕
We screen out
  • ✕ Desk workers & office backgrounds
  • ✕ Chefs, cooks, food service
  • ✕ Bus drivers & transit workers
  • ✕ Candidates unwilling to commit to the physical demands
  • ✕ Anyone who won't complete background + drug test
